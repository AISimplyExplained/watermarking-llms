# Watermarking LLMs Primer
Welcome to our educational series where we delve into the innovative research paper titled **"A Watermark for Large Language Models"** by John Kirchenbauer, Jonas Geiping, Yuxin Wen, Jonathan Katz, Ian Miers, and Tom Goldstein, published at ICML 2023. This section is designed to simplify and effectively explain the contents of the paper, making the complex concepts accessible to all readers.<br/>
